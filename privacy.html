Privacy Policy — SpotWar

Last updated: May 31, 2026

SpotWar (“the App”) is operated by the SpotWar team. This policy describes how we handle information when you use the mobile app.

Information we collect

  • Account: email address and display name when you register
  • Gameplay: scores, XP, coins, energy, achievements, match history, and leaderboard data
  • Device & usage: identifiers and analytics collected by Firebase and Google AdMob

How we use information

  • Provide gameplay, matchmaking, and account features
  • Save and sync your progress
  • Show optional advertisements (rewarded and interstitial)
  • Improve stability and fix bugs

Third-party services

We use:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, cloud database, and realtime features
  • Google AdMob — advertising

These providers process data under their own privacy policies: Google Privacy Policy.

Children

SpotWar is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your choices

  • You may use guest mode without registering
  • Adjust sound, music, vibration, and notifications in app Settings
  • Delete your account in the app: Settings → Delete Account (or Delete Guest Data for guests)

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

Overview

SpotWar (“the App”) is operated by the SpotWar team. This policy describes how we handle information when you use the mobile app on iPhone or iPad.

Information We Collect

  • Account: email address and display name when you register
  • Gameplay: scores, XP, coins, energy, achievements, match history, and leaderboard data
  • Device & usage: identifiers and analytics collected by Firebase and Google AdMob

How We Use Information

  • Provide gameplay, matchmaking, and account features
  • Save and sync your progress across sessions
  • Show optional advertisements (rewarded and interstitial)
  • Improve stability and fix bugs

Third-Party Services

We use the following providers, each operating under their own privacy policies:

🔥

Firebase

Authentication, cloud database, and realtime features by Google

📢

Google AdMob

Advertising SDK — rewarded and interstitial ad formats

See the Google Privacy Policy for details on how these services process data.

Children

SpotWar is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Choices

  • Use guest mode without registering — no email required
  • Adjust sound, music, vibration, and notifications in app Settings
  • Delete your account in the app: Settings → Delete Account (or Delete Guest Data for guests). This permanently removes your account and all associated data.

Common Questions

  • Online play not working? Sign in with a registered account — guests cannot play online. Make sure you have a stable internet connection before entering a match.
  • Out of energy? Energy refills automatically (+1 per hour, up to 10). You can also watch optional rewarded ads to gain extra energy instantly.
  • Forgot your password? Tap "Forgot password" on the login screen inside the app. A reset link will be sent to your registered email.
  • How do I delete my account or data? Open the app → Settings → Delete Account (registered users) or Delete Guest Data (guests). This permanently removes your account and all associated data.
  • Progress not syncing across devices? Make sure you're signed in with the same account on each device. Guest progress is local only and cannot be transferred.

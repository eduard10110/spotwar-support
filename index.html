SpotWar — Support

SpotWar is a spot-the-difference puzzle game with practice levels and online PvP battles.

Need help? Email us:

spotwars.differencebattle@gmail.com

We aim to reply within 48 hours.

Common questions

  • Online play not working? Sign in with an account (guests cannot play online). Check your internet connection.
  • No energy? Energy refills over time (+1 per hour, max 10). Optional rewarded ads may grant extra energy.
  • Password reset? Use “Forgot password” on the login screen in the app.
  • Delete account or data? Open the app → Settings → Delete Account (registered users) or Delete Guest Data (guests). Permanently removes your account and associated data.

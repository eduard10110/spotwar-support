How it works

  1. Compare two images side by side and tap the differences you spot.
  2. Score points for every correct find — wrong taps cost you time.
  3. Beat the clock in Practice, or outscore your rival in Online PvP.
  4. Level up, collect coins, and compete on the global leaderboard.
